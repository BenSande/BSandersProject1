Fajita Frenzy

Amazing fajita recipe for the whole family

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts

  • Fajita seasoning mix

  • Light olive oil

  • Variety of colored bell peppers

  • Any type of onions

  • Sea salt & black pepper

  • Lime wedges

  • Tortillas

How to Prepare

  1. Season chicken breasts with fajita seasoning. Sprinkle it on both sides of the chicken breasts and use your hands to rub in gently, covering the whole piece of chicken.

  2. Dice the onions. Cut the onion in half from root end to root end. Place the cut side of the onion face down on the cutting board and slice from the outside of the onion going toward the center.

  3. Slice the bell peppers. Slice off the top of the bell peppers, removing the stem. Place the cut side down on the cutting board and cut straight down into four sections. Removing any white stem and seeds from the middle of the sections. Slice each section into strips.

How to Cook

  1. Sear chicken. Heat oil in a large skillet until it shimmers. Add chicken breasts and cook for about 5-7 minutes per side, until cooked through. Remove chicken and cover with foil to keep warm.

  2. Use a meat thermometer to check temperature. Chicken is ready when it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees F

  3. Cook peppers and onions. Add bell peppers and onions to the pan. Saute for about 4-5 minutes, until soft and browned. Season with sea salt and black pepper.

  4. Slice chicken. While peppers and onions are cooking, slice the chicken breasts against the grain.
